### PITCH (Hiring subs version)
"We've got a couple of projects coming up and we're vetting subcontractors now before we break ground. I wanted to reach out directly to see if you're taking on new work and if it might be a good fit."

---

### PITCH (Selling to subs version)
"We help subcontractors like yourself get connected with more general contractors and project leads in your area — without having to chase bids all day."

---

### OBJECTION HANDLERS

**"Not interested"**
"Totally get it — I won't waste your time. Could I just send over a quick email with more details so you have it when the timing's better?"

**"We're too busy"**
"That's actually a great sign — busy crews are good crews. I'll keep your info on file for future projects. What's the best email to send work requests to?"

**"Who are you again?"**
"Vega Apps — we're a construction development and project management company working in [STATE]. We vet and partner with local subs directly rather than going through the big bid boards."

---

### CLOSE
"Awesome — I'll shoot you a quick email so you have our contact info. And if a project comes up that fits what you do, we'll reach out directly. Sound good?"
